Trax

Trax allows you to create a Spotify playlist collaboratively with others. Unlike the traditional Spotify collaborative playlist, Trax allows non-Spotify users to add to a playlist and only requires the original playlist creator to login to Spotify. With minimal barriers to participating, it's an excellent way to create a new playlist for a roadtrip, party, hackathon, or team project.
